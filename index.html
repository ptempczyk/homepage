About me

I graduated Physics at University of Warsaw and now I am a Ph.D student at faculty of Mathematics, Informatics and Mechanics under supervision of Marek Cygan. I have 10+ years of experience in applied statistics and machine learning and in recent years I decided to focus on academic research on neural networks and Bayesian statistics. I am also a co-founder and CTO at deeptale.ai, where we do R&D on computer vision problems and build deeply personalized products using generative models. Apart from studies and work, I support machine learning community in Poland with my friends from Polish National Institute for Machine Learning Foundation (OPIUM). After work I spend my time with my wife and two daughters, ride a dirt bike and a supermoto, and play all kinds of Souls-like board and computer games.

Accomplishments

Here I present my work during PhD and some other scientific projects I am/was involved in:

LIDL: Local Intrinsic Dimension estimation using approximate Likelihood

My paper with R. Michaluk, Ł. Garncarek, P. Spurek, J. Tabor and A. Goliński was accepted as a long presentation at ICLM 2022. I was presented before as a poster on ICLR 2021 GTRL Workshop and at ML in PL 2021 conference as a contributed talk. .

n-CPS: Generalising Cross Pseudo Supervision to n networks for Semi-Supervised Semantic Segmentation

Joint work with Dominik Filipiak and Marek Cygan on semi-supervised semantic segmentation during my work at AI Clearing.

NeurIPS 2021 BEETL Competition: Benchmarks for EEG Transfer Learning

We took 3-rd place in the competition with Maciej Śliwowski and Vincent Rouanne. Our work was presented during the talk at the NeurIPS 2021 BEETL competition workshop.

Reinforcement learning based brain-computer interface

Joint work with Maciej Śliwowski. Poster presentation at EEML 2020 and best poster award in reinforcement learning category.

Acta Schola Automata Polonica

I am one of the main writers and editor at a blog about machine learning research.

People's Reinforcement Learning

I was the main developer in python package for reinforcement learning agent building and testing.
